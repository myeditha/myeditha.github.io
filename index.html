    What I'm Listening To Right Now

    Untrue

    Burial

    As pretty much everyone around me can attest, my recent obsession has been unpacking the sounds and experiences in this remarkable album, and understanding how it affects others.

    Unlike other great albums I've heard, this one doesn't revel in or even acknowledge its own greatness. It creaks and wobbles and wheezes like a rickety wooden rollercoaster. Built on top of skeletal beats and a rich bass sound and topped off by ghostly samples resonating like echoes in a forest, each and every track is striking, haunting, even strangely uplifting. As you listen to each track, it feels like you're watching a mini arthouse movie, stripped of all of the symbolism and pretension and intellectual messages until all you have left is a feeling - an aching and melancholy and optimistic feeling that despite your past, there's a world of beauty and wonder out there to discover. Critics classify this album as "dubstep", but the difference in vibe between this and music produced by an artist like Skrillex could not be more apparent.

    Favorite tracks: Ghost Hardware, Archangel, Etched Headplate, Raver
